About Me

Hi, my name is Darren Maxvel, but you can call me Maxvel. I live in West Jakarta and I'm interested in applying for the position of ship captain at your company. I graduated from the Tokyo University of Marine Science and Technology with a degree in Nautical Science in 2031.

  • Name: Darren
  • Age: 23
  • Occupation: Sailor

I am a highly motivated and dedicated individual with a strong passion for maritime affairs. Over the past few years, I have honed my skills in navigation, ship management, and maritime leadership. I am accustomed to working effectively in teams and ensuring the safety and well-being of all crew members aboard.

My experience at sea has equipped me with the ability to make quick and decisive decisions, particularly in emergency situations and unpredictable weather conditions. As an aspiring ship captain, I envision myself leading a vessel with utmost responsibility, ensuring safe and efficient voyages, and fostering strong teamwork among the crew.

  • Route Planning and Optimization I specialize in developing detailed voyage plans that optimize fuel efficiency, manage time effectively, and ensure safety, guaranteeing smooth and timely travel across various sea routes.
  • Cargo Operations Management I oversee the entire cargo operations process, from loading and securing to unloading. My focus is on ensuring that all operations comply with safety regulations and industry standards for efficient and safe transport.
  • Emergency Drill Coordination I coordinate and conduct regular emergency drills, preparing the crew for various scenarios. This enhances our safety preparedness and ensures compliance with maritime safety regulations.
  • Environmental Compliance and Sustainability Practices I implement and monitor practices that align with environmental regulations, promoting sustainability initiatives such as waste management, pollution prevention, and conservation efforts.

My Journey

My curiosity has always led me to explore new places. As I traveled around Jakarta, I couldn't shake the feeling that there was more to the world. The ocean, with its mysteries and vastness, seemed like the ultimate destination for a curious mind like mine.

  • Early Years

    I started my life as a typical kid living in a village in North Sumatra. I loved riding my bike and exploring new places. Everything changed when I moved to Jakarta and made new friends, discovering new hobbies along the way.

  • Education and Training

    During high school, I became increasingly determined to become a ship captain. This dream motivated me to study harder. Thanks to my hard work and family support, I was awarded a full scholarship to Tokyo University of Marine Science and Technology. I chose this university because of its excellent reputation in marine science and the opportunity to further develop my Japanese language skills, which I had started learning in junior high.

  • Work Experience

    I wrapped up my degree at Tokyo University of Marine Science and Technology in 2031. Since then, I've been honing my seafaring skills on a variety of vessels, from small fishing boats in the Pacific Ocean to massive cargo ships that have traversed the Atlantic. Each journey has provided me with invaluable experience and a deeper appreciation for the vastness of the oceans. In addition to my commercial shipping experience, I've also been involved in several research expeditions. I've participated in studies on marine biodiversity, climate change, and ocean currents. These expeditions have allowed me to contribute to scientific knowledge and gain a deeper understanding of the delicate balance of marine ecosystems.
